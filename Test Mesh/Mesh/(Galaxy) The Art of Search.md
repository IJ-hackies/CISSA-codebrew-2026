# The Art of Search

How do you find a needle in an exponential haystack? This galaxy charts the journey from brute-force exploration to the sophisticated heuristic methods that make planning practical. It begins at the memory wall — the brutal arithmetic showing that systematic search devours RAM faster than patience — and traces the inventions that tame it: iterative deepening reclaiming memory, heuristics focusing effort, A* combining actual cost with optimistic estimates. Then it descends into the engine room of modern planning: problem relaxation, where hard problems are simplified to produce the estimates that guide search. The eight puzzle is cracked open, the logistics truck delivers its package, and the TSP reveals that even relaxed problems can be hard. At the journey's end, the FF heuristic emerges — the practical champion that extracts actual plans from relaxed worlds and uses them to solve the real ones.
